Design and Creative Solutions

We deliver visually stunning and cohesive designs that align with the brand's ethos and message. Our design services include:

Graphic design for digital and print media
  • Graphic design for digital and print media
  • Corporate identity and branding design
  • Website and user interface (UI) design
  • Infographics, business reports and visual storytelling
  • Packaging design and product branding

Strategic Communication Consulting

We offer comprehensive communication consulting services to align business goals with effective communication strategies. These include:

  • Brand strategy development
  • Corporate communication strategy/planning
  • Change management communication

Corporate Events and Communications

We provide end-to-end event management and communication services to ensure successful event execution. Services include:

  • Event conceptualization and planning
  • Media coverage and press coordination
  • Branding and promotions
  • Post-event communication and reporting

Corporate Branding and Identity Development

We create and develop strong, memorable brand identities that resonate with target audiences. Services include:

  • Brand identity and logo design
  • Corporate visual communication strategies
  • Brand positioning and message development
  • Rebranding and brand refresh strategies

Communication for Development (C4D)

We work with development agencies, NGOs, and government bodies to craft and deliver communication strategies that drive social change and development impact. Our C4D services include:

  • Results documentation and story telling
  • Designing and executing communication strategies for development initiatives
  • Community outreach and stakeholder engagement
  • Advocacy & awareness campaigns for health, education, gender equality, and sustainability e.t.c
  • Capacity building and training in communication for development

Photography and Video Production

We help organizations build powerful videos and stories that drive change, build trust and connect brands with their audiences emotionally. Our services include:

  • Developing comprehensive marketing and communications videos
  • Video conception, production, and editing.
  • Professional photograph
  • Short videos and Documentaries
